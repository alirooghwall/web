# Product Images

Please add your real product photos to this folder with the following names:

1. **health-product.jpg** - Health and wellness product image
2. **beauty-product.jpg** - Beauty and skincare product image  
3. **organic-product.jpg** - Organic product image

## Image Requirements:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Recommended size: 800x600 pixels (4:3 ratio)
- File size: Keep under 500KB for fast loading
- Quality: High resolution, clear product photos

## Fallback:
If images are not found, the website will display the company logo as a placeholder.

## How to Add Images:
1. Rename your product photos to match the names above
2. Copy them to this `products/` folder
3. Refresh the website to see your images

**Note:** You can update the Google Maps location in the contact section by getting the embed code from Google Maps and replacing it in index.html (search for "iframe" in the contact section).
